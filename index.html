About hotel Pinangsia

Pinangsia Hotel, located in Jakarta's Old Town area, has a long history dating back to the Dutch colonial era when Batavia was a trade center. Established to accommodate traders and travelers, the hotel endured various changes following Indonesia's independence in 1945. Today, Pinangsia Hotel remains a historic icon in Jakarta, reflecting the city's development from the colonial era to modern times.

Our Vision

Pinangsia Hotel's vision is to become a leading historic hotel in Jakarta, offering an authentic and classy stay experience with a touch of classic ambiance and top-notch service.

Our Mision

Its mission is to provide friendly and professional service to every guest, preserve and maintain the hotel's cultural heritage and historical values, offer comfortable and modern facilities without losing its historic character, and become the destination of choice for travelers seeking to experience the uniqueness of Jakarta's Old Town.
